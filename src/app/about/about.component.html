About the "Disiplina Muna" Campaign

Empowering Fernandinos Through Discipline

Our Mission

To uplift the Filipino culture of discipline, which is seen as crucial for achieving sustainable development and building a stronger nation. The "Disiplina Muna" campaign is a national advocacy program initiated by the Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG). It aims to instill a culture of discipline among Filipinos and encourage local government units (LGUs) to take the lead in promoting community participation and compliance with laws and regulations.
